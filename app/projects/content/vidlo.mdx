I started a YouTube channel a while back and the main friction i had each video was how to come up with the idea, and flesh out the script to make it entertaining, writing and writing to be engaging are two very different things.

There was no soltuion out there that worked the exact way i wanted it to and had all the features I was looking for so I just made it myself. Vidlo can help you come up with ideas and titles, and help you write your video script. As well as having a full analytics dashboard so you can see whats going on, with AI Insights to point out what's working and not working.

If you have a YouTube channel, give it a go for free and see how you like it!
